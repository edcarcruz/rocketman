How to Play

Welcome to Rocketman! Your mission is to guess the hidden word related to space exploration and get our Rocketman on the ship! Here's how to play:

  • A hidden word related to space will be displayed with underscores representing missing letters.
  • Click on the on-screen keyboard buttons to guess a letter.
  • If the guessed letter is correct, it will be revealed in the word.
  • If the guessed letter is incorrect, it will be added to the list of wrong letters, and the rocketship will get one step closer to takeoff.
  • You have a total of 6 attempts to guess the word correctly. If you run out of attempts, you lose the game and the rocket blasts off without our Rocketman.
  • If you successfully guess the entire word, congratulations, your Rocketman will be off to space!

Click the "Try Again" button to play a new round after winning or losing.
